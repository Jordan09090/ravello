Process

What to expect

01

Consultation

Your tan begins with a personalised consultation. Once you arrive, we take a moment to chat about your event or occasion, the colour you’ll be wearing, how dark you’d like to go, and how long you have to develop your tan, whether you're after a quick rinse or want to leave it on overnight. Your natural skin tone and undertones are carefully assessed so we can select the perfect solution from our professional range.

02

Barrier

Barrier cream is then applied before tanning. This ensures an even, seamless result by preventing excess colour build-up on dry areas like hands, feet, elbows, and knees, giving you a smoother, more natural finish.

03

Application

Your custom spray tan is then applied with precision. Every tan is thorough, even, and tailored specifically to you.

04

Finish

We finish with a full dry-off and setting powder. This locks in your tan, reduces stickiness, and allows you to leave feeling comfortable, confident, and flawless.
